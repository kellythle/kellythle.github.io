Italy's Cuisine

Italian cuisine is deeply influenced by its geographic position especially the plentiful supply of fresh seafood from the
Mediterranean Sea. While you can sample Italian cuisine all over the world nowadays, it is worthwhile understanding
what makes Italian food so popular the world over.
