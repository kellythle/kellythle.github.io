Korea's Points of Interest

Here are four of of Korea's many points of interest:

Point of Interest About Picture
N Seoul Tower The N Seoul Tower is located in Seoul and attracts tourists because of its incredible city view at night. It is located on top of the Namsan Mountain and has been featured in many Korean dramas. The tower also has cafés, restaurants, and more.
Gyeongbokgung Palace Gyeongbokgung Palace is located in Seoul and was once the home to the royal family during the Joseon Dynasty. Its meaning is "a place where the new king can enjoy prosperity of great blessings." The palace has multiple buildings which have different designs that each serve a purpose. There are also cultural performances done on the palace grounds.
The Garden of Morning Calm The Garden of Morning Calm is located in Gyeonggi-do and is the image of a beautiful Korean botanical garden. It shows the beauty of the country through the plants and is simply a relaxing place to walk around and enjoy nature. The garden also offers forest bathing and is popular for being featured in Korea dramas. A lighting festival also takes place during the winter, where lights illuminate the garden at night.
Myeong-dong Myeong-dong is located in Seoul and is known for its street food and its fashion and shopping stores. This area is popular among young people, as it contains the latest fashion trends. Korea is known as a homeland to cosmetics, and this area sells plenty of branded cosmetics. Myeong-dong also contains many famous restaurants.
